  • Martina Reverberi – 24, from Reggio Emilia, Italy. Master’s degree in Computer Engineering (AI) at UniPR.

Erasmus Experience

This is our very first Erasmus adventure and also the first time we are living in Seville. We are really excited about this opportunity, which we see as an important step for both our personal growth and our professional development. Although the beginning has not always been easy, adapting to a new city and culture, we are motivated and ready to make the most of this experience. Studying Computer Engineering in an international environment gives us the chance to broaden our perspectives and improve our skills in a field that is constantly evolving.

Why we chose Loyola University

We chose Loyola University in Seville because of its international environment and the chance to experience a different academic approach. The university offers us the opportunity to grow not only in our studies but also in a multicultural setting, meeting students and professors from all over the world. Loyola felt like the right place to combine education, culture, and personal discovery.

Discovering Seville

Living in Seville is already a unique experience. The city welcomes us with its warm atmosphere, stunning architecture, and lively traditions. From the beauty of Plaza de España to the charming streets of Triana, every corner of Seville feels full of history and energy. We enjoy exploring the local culture, trying new food, and experiencing the everyday rhythm of Andalusian life.

Challenges and Growth

As with every new beginning, there have been challenges. Adapting to a new country, language, and lifestyle is not always easy. However, these challenges are also opportunities: they teach us resilience, flexibility, and open-mindedness. Every day we feel we are growing, both as students and as individuals, learning to face situations outside of our comfort zone.

Looking Ahead

We believe this Erasmus experience will leave a lasting mark on our future. It will strengthen our academic path, improve our language skills, and enrich our personal and professional outlook. Looking ahead, we hope to bring back to Italy not only knowledge and skills but also unforgettable memories and friendships that will stay with us for life.
